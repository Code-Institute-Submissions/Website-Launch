Who Are We ???...

We are an English rock band formed in Manchester in 1991. Highly successful both domestically and internationally consistently filling stadiums worldwide and headlining festivals.

Our repotoire extends to playing live in any format large or small having performed at numerous Weddings and Chritmas Parties from superstar celebrity weddings right through to smaller more intimate venues.

Watch Us Play

Knebworth

It was the concert that defined an era. Over two days at Hertfordshire's Knebworth House, we played to 250,000 people. Riding high on the success of 1994's Definitely Maybe the concert found the band at the peak of our powers.

Earls Court

This performance drew in 20,000 die-hard fans. Earls Court was superb as we were doing stadium performances on pure instinct. Earls Court was just after the release of Morning Glory. It was pure storming hedonism.

Wembley

April 27 and 28 saw Oasis headline our first ever stadium show. Fittingly, we took place at Wembley. “Wembley was where we all used to go as kidsto watch football,” so to perform live here was truly sensational.


Biography

Noel Gallagher

Noel Thomas David Gallagher (born 29 May 1967) is a British singer, songwriter and guitarist. He served as the lead guitarist, co-lead vocalist and principal songwriter of the English rock band Oasis. Raised in Burnage, Manchester, Gallagher began learning guitar at the age of thirteen.

Liam Gallagher

William John Paul "Liam" Gallagher (born 21 September 1972) is an English singer and songwriter. Liam rose to fame as the lead singer of the rock band Oasis. His eccentric dress sense, distinctive singing style, and abrasive attitude have been the subject of commentary in the press; he remains one of the most recognisable figures in modern British music.
