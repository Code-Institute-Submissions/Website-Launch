Who Are We ???...

We are an English rock band formed in Manchester in 1991. Highly successful both domestically and internationally consistently filling stadiums worldwide and headlining festivals.

Our repotoire extends to playing live in any format large or small having performed at numerous Weddings and Christmas Parties from superstar celebrity weddings right through to smaller more intimate venues.

Check out the rest of the site where you can view some of our back catalogue and a selection of our live performances.

Watch Us Live

Knebworth

It was the concert that defined an era. Over two days at Hertfordshire's Knebworth House, we played to 250,000 people. Riding high on the success of 1994's Definitely Maybe the concert found the band at the peak of our powers.

Earls Court

This performance drew in 20,000 die-hard fans. Earls Court was superb as we were doing stadium performances on pure instinct. Earls Court was just after the release of Morning Glory. It was pure storming hedonism.


Wembley

April 27 and 28 saw the band headline our first ever stadium show. Fittingly, it took place at Wembley. The Stadium was where we all used to go as kids to watch football, so to perform live here over multiple shows was truly sensational.





Meet The Band

Noel Gallagher

Hi, im Noel Thomas David Gallagher born 29 May 1967. Im a British singer, songwriter and guitarist. I served as the lead guitarist, co-lead vocalist and principal songwriter for Oasis.


Liam Gallagher

William John Paul "Liam" Gallagher rose to fame as the lead singer of Oasis. The eccentric dress sense, distinctive singing style, and abrasive attitude have been the subject of commentary in the press however remains one of the most recognisable figures in modern British music.
